WELCOME REMARKS BY THE RESIDENT DIRECTOR

It is with great joy and excitement that I welcome you all to this special occasion at SEROMA Christian High School.
As the Resident Director, I am truly honored to see so many bright and eager faces, ready to embark on a new chapter of learning and growth.
At SEROMA Christian High, we strive not only for academic excellence but also for nurturing character and building a strong foundation in faith.
Our mission is to help each student discover their true potential, develop a love for learning, and become responsible, compassionate individuals in society.
We are committed to providing a safe, supportive, and inspiring environment for all our students, where they can grow in both knowledge and wisdom.
Whether you are joining us for the first time or returning for another year, we are here to guide and support you every step of the way. Once again,
welcome to the SEROMA Christian High School community. Let’s work together to make this year a truly remarkable one.
May this journey be filled with learning, growth, and success! Thank you, and God bless you all.
Resident Director: Mrs Amelia Kyambadde

About our School

We are one of the best, most diverse secondary schools in the Uganda. SEROMA was ranked among the nine four-star schools in Uganda
according to the 2009 Ministry of Education and Sports ranking of private secondary schools.

We provide a carefully assembled package that includes the academics, co-curricular and the Christian social life well guided by our qualified staff.
Our Christian and result-oriented approach to issues has proved appropriate.
